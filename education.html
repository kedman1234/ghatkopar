Scholarship

Educations has become expensive and for deserving candidate this is a big Challenge for students too. Samaj try to motivate this young deserving achieves by contributing scholarship fees. 150 school students and 90 college students.

Every year Samaj donates more than 15 lakh Rupees in this social cause.

Adoption of school

Samaj has adopted "Shree gujrati Samaj Vidyalaya " Samaj supports this activity by providing

  • School fees
  • Text books
  • Note books
  • Uniforms
  • Bus fees

The activity is entirely sponsored under CSR activity of shri SOMALAL KHEMREM VYAS & FAMILY.

Higher Education

Under CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY (CSR) Samaj has under taken project for higher education scholarship program. This fund will be utilised for following activities

  • Engineering students
  • Medical students
  • MBA
  • Post Graduation

This project will be on the terms of loan provision to the student which will be refunded in instalment on completion of studies.
